Aqua, Caprylic/Capric Triglyceride, Sodium Benzoate, Potassuim Sorbate, Acrylamide/Sodium Acrylate Copolimer, Trideceth-6, Mineral Oil, Allantoin, Aloe Vera Gel, Disodium EDTA, Citric Acid, Didecyldimethylammonium Chloride, Parfum
Store in the manufacturer’s hermetically sealed packaging in covered warehouses at temperatures from 0 ° C to +30 ° C, in places inaccessible to children, away from heaters, open flames, direct sunlight. After the expiration date, the use of napkins is prohibited. The time of effective treatment of the skin is at least 1 minute.
Date of manufacture (day, month, year), batch number are indicated on the package. Shelf life: 24 months from the date of manufacture in unopened packaging of the manufacturer.
